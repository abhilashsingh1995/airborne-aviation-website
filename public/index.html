Why students choose us

Choose a teacher, not a brand.

A ground school is not a building or a logo. It is the person standing in front of the whiteboard. At Airborne, that person is the same one for every class.

01

The captain teaches every batch

No assistants, no rotating faculty. Capt. Navrang Singh personally takes navigation, meteorology, and tech general. You get the real one.

02

25-student cap, ever

Bigger batches make a profitable academy. They do not make a good pilot. We cap at 25 because that's the largest room one teacher can actually hold attention in.

03

First-attempt obsession

Each repeat exam costs you fees, time, and confidence. Our entire study plan is built backwards from the DGCA paper, not forwards from a textbook.

04

Transparent before you pay

Fees, timings, expectations and what we will not promise — all on the table in your first counselling call. No selling, no surprises.
